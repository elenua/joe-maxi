Welcome

Taxi Drivers of Ireland coming together to operate their own app.



Lower cost to the driver means, the driver becomes more available to the public. Drivers usually turn off apps due to the high percentages they pay to Hailo. Passengers are wondering, “why are there no taxis working?” They are working, they just don’t want to pay high commissions during peak hours.

We will target many different types of customers. Allowing customers tell us where they plan to book taxis at Airports and Train Stations for example.

We will continue to provide a professional taxi service to the taxi users of Ireland. We will provide this service using our own app. The Joe Maxi App.

Drivers are going to experience a serious bump in their earnings. Joe Maxi will go into “busy mode” during peak hours. Drivers will only be only offered jobs within 1 kilometre of where they are. So, on a busy night, you will stay on hire all night.

Question time : Why do existing apps work ? People get taxis easily ? Yes ! 10 out of 10 for realising that. Customers have to use the taxi app that has the available taxis. Thats the reason why we will have a changeover date. In the changeover week, we launch our own app by word of mouth and some radio advertisements. We will tell the public, Joe Maxi is the taxi drivers own app, and the new way to order taxis. Make no mistake about it, this is going to be a revolutionary style take over of our own industry.

Customers will use the Joe Maxi App, as it will be the only App where people can find available taxis.
